Welcome to our Claremont gold buy services

The Diamond Center in Claremont, California is known throughout the region as a fair buyer of gold and fine jewelry. We can offer a buy or trade-in price. All estimates must be made in person at our store. Make an appointment to meet with our buyer. We are excited to meet you!

What to Expect

When you bring in your items to be assessed, please bring any paperwork, prior appraisals, or other documentation with you. Our buyer will take a look at your items and make you two offers, a buy offer and a trade offer. If you select the buy offer, you will receive a check for that amount. If you select a trade offer, you will receive store credit (typically higher than the buy offer) to use at The Diamond Center. You are not under any obligation to accept either offer.

We buy:

  • Gold & fine jewelry
  • Sterling silver (must be marked Sterling, SS, or 925)
  • Diamond jewelry
